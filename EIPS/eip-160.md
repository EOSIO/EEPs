### Parameters
- `FORK_BLKNUM`: 2,675,000
- `CHAIN_ID`: 1

### Specification

If `block.number >= FORK_BLKNUM`, increase the gas cost of EXP from 10 + 10 per byte in the exponent to 10 + 50 per byte in the exponent.
